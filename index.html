our story

From a Cottage Kitchen, With Care

Art Cottage Bakery is an entirely from-scratch home bakery, where every cake, cookie, and muffin is made by hand in small batches. There are no shortcuts and no mixes, only good ingredients, patience, and a painter's eye.

We believe a bake should be as beautiful as it is delicious, and that the two should never be at odds. That is what we mean by taste that compliments integrity.

Tessa
