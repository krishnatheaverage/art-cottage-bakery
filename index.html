Where the Bakery Gets Its Name

Art Cottage Bakery is led by a working oil painter, muralist, and book illustrator. Every cake, cookie, and loaf is made from scratch and finished by hand, with the same eye she brings to a canvas. Here, the "art" in the name is literal.

"Where taste compliments integrity."
